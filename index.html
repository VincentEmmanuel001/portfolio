About Me

Hello I am Emmanuel! I am a full-stack web developer from Niger State, Nigeria. I specialize in creating beautiful interface websites with dark background, which makes the site look both stylish and clean.

If I am not coding I am travelling. Travelling helps open my mind. I realize that there's no one way to live life. Meeting people from other places shows that the world view is not the same as everyone else. The different setting helps me discover and consider fresh ideas I hadn't thought before. By being exposed to new places, people and cultures, I developed a wider view of the world.

I polled my friends and family from all over the world on why they love to travel. Here are their responses:

"I think the exposure to new places and new people can be really reviving personally and also eye opening to see how other people live. To see how life functions, whether human or natural life, in other places is really humbling. It's easy to have your status quo at home, but as soon as you're in a new place - all bets are off. You can do anything, and are so willing to try new stuff to push your own boundaries purely because you're in a new place." -Dove

"I love to travel because not only is it the ultimate adventure but it also exposes you to new types of people, different ways of living, and opens up your mind. I just like to walk around in beautiful places that resemble the shire from Lord of the Rings while exploring new culinary terrains." -Paul
